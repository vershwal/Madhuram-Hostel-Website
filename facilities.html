Covid Safety Facilities

Our key staff will maintain proper Social Distancing Guidelines. In addition to that, we will be equipped with Masks and sufficient Sanitizers.

Each room has been sanitized and it will be sanitized again before a student moves in. Common Areas in Hostel will be sanitized regularly.

Each support staff (Guard, Maids, etc.) will be wearing face masks. Additionally they will be equipped with Face Shields and Gloves.

Hostel is located in close proximity to Allen, Library, Medical Facility and Supermarket. Reducing the Risk of Interaction with the crowd.
