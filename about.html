About the Directors

Bhuwnesh Lahoty

Mr. Lahoty is the Owner and the Director of Madhuram Group of Hostels. He is the most reputed architectural consultant of the city and is the founder of the firm, Lahoty Associates. He designed the majority of Coaching Institutes in Kota like Allen Institute, Career Point, Vibrant Academy and designed over 100 hostels in Kota. Having the perfect in and out hostel experience, he manages Madhuram Hostels with staff that is standing in his support since past 15 years. He looks after the hostel Management and takes care of the maintainence.

Pranav Lahoty

Pranav graduated from IIT Roorkee in 2016 and since then he is dedicated in making Madhuram Girls Hostel a better place for students to live. By constantly bringing in new facilities and upgradation in hostel, his aim is to make the hostel the best one in kota where the environment is stress free and students can feel like at home. He solves the issues that students are facing and he plans all the events that we organize. He is the go-to person for every student or parent if they are facing any problem.
